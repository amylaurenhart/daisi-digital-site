The process

How we work

Here’s what working with us actually looks like.

  1. 01

    Discovery call

    A 30-minute chat about your business, your audience, and what you actually want social to do for you. If we’re not the right fit, we’ll tell you. If we are, we’ll send a proposal for you to review.

  2. 02

    Strategy and audit

    We audit your current social, research your competitors, and build a content plan mapped to your business goals.

  3. 03

    Content and community

    We handle content creation, scheduling, publishing, and engagement. You choose how hands-on to be, from filming your own content with our direction to handing the whole thing over. Shared calendar and a direct chat channel so you always know what’s happening.

  4. 04

    Reports and adjustments

    Monthly reports on what’s working and what’s not. We flag opportunities and shifts as they come up, not just when it’s time to review.
